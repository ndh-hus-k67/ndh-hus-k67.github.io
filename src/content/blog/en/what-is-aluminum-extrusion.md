## 1. What are aluminum extrusion accessories?

**Aluminum extrusion accessories** are mechanical components used to connect, fix or support the movement of aluminum extrusion profiles during assembly.

Using standardized accessories allows aluminum frame structures to be:

- Faster to assemble  
- Weld-free  
- Easy to disassemble and redesign  
- Cost-effective for industrial applications  

Today, **industrial aluminum extrusion accessories** are commonly used in:

- Machine frames  
- Conveyor systems  
- Industrial workstations  
- Robotics systems  
- Automation equipment

Using the correct **aluminum profile connectors and accessories** also improves the rigidity and stability of the entire structure.

## 2. Ball slider for aluminum extrusion

A **ball slider for aluminum extrusion** allows components to slide smoothly along the aluminum profile slot.

### Key features

- Smooth movement thanks to rolling ball elements  
- Low friction during operation  
- High durability  
- Easy installation inside the aluminum slot  

Because of this design, **ball sliders** are often used in systems that require precise linear movement.

### Common applications

Ball sliders are widely used in:

- Sliding aluminum doors  
- Automated machine mechanisms  
- Adjustable positioning systems  
- Linear guiding mechanisms

Using a ball slider ensures **smooth and stable movement in industrial applications**.

An **anti-rotation hidden bracket** connects two aluminum profiles internally and prevents unwanted rotation in the frame structure.

- Prevents profile rotation  
- Clean and aesthetic design

### Industrial applications

Anti-rotation brackets are commonly used in:

- Industrial machine frames
