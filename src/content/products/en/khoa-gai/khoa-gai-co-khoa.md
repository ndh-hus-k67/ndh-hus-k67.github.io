  content: |
    The padlockable toggle latch is a mechanical latch designed with an additional lock hole that allows users to secure the latch with a separate padlock or security seal. This design provides an extra level of protection for equipment enclosures and containers.

    </br></br>
    When used without a padlock, the product operates like a standard toggle latch with a lever mechanism that allows quick and convenient opening and closing. When security is required, a padlock can be inserted through the lock hole to prevent the latch from being opened.

    </br></br>
    Padlockable toggle latches are commonly manufactured from stainless steel or zinc plated steel to ensure strong mechanical performance and corrosion resistance in industrial environments. High-grade versions may also use stainless steel 316 for marine or chemical applications.

    </br></br>
    Thanks to the combination of quick fastening and additional security, this latch is widely used in industrial equipment, electrical cabinets, transport boxes and containers.

    </br></br>
    Typical technical specifications include:

    </br></br>
    - Material: Stainless steel 304, stainless steel 316 or zinc plated steel.
    </br>
    - Mechanism: Toggle lever with integrated padlock hole.
    </br>
    - Function: Quick locking with optional padlock security.
    </br>
    - Installation: Surface mounting with screws.
    </br>
    - Applications: Electrical cabinets, equipment enclosures, transport containers and tool boxes.
