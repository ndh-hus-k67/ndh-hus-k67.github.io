  content: |
    Spring ball T-nuts are aluminum-profile accessories commonly used for assembling industrial aluminum frames. They work like a nut with an internal thread and are shaped to match the slot of aluminum profiles.
    The spring-loaded ball presses against the slot wall to increase friction and retention, helping prevent the nut from falling out when the profile is tilted or vertical.
    </br></br>
