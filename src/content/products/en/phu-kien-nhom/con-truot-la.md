  content: |
    Leaf T-nuts are aluminum-profile accessories commonly used for assembling industrial aluminum frames. They act like a nut with an internal thread and are shaped to match aluminum profile slots for convenient assembly.
    </br></br>
    The elastic leaf/spring design helps press the nut against the slot wall to improve grip and reduce drop-out during assembly, especially when the profile is tilted or vertical.
    </br></br>
