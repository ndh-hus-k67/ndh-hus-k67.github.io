  content: |
    One-end anti-rotation hidden brackets are aluminum-profile accessories widely used for assembling industrial aluminum frame systems. The bracket features a single threaded end that matches the aluminum profile slot geometry for convenient assembly.
    </br></br>
    Anti-rotation teeth help it bite into the slot wall, creating a strong connection and preventing rotation. The bracket is pressed into the profile slot to form a reliable, threaded mounting point.
    </br></br>
    <h2 class="mb-3 text-lg font-semibold text-neutral-900 dark:text-neutral-100">Interference when installing aluminum profile end caps?</h2>
    <strong>Problem with double-end hidden brackets:</strong>
    </br>
    Double-end brackets lock both sides → they can interfere when adding end caps or when installing recessed acrylic covers.
    </br></br>
    <strong>✅ Solution with one-end bracket:</strong>
    </br></br>
    <ul class="list-disc pl-6 space-y-1">
      <li>Prevents interference when installing aluminum profile end caps</li>
      <li>No need to cut/modify acrylic covers</li>
      <li>More flexible, convenient installation</li>
      <li>Saves assembly time and effort</li>
    </ul>
    </br></br>
    <h2 class="mb-3 text-lg font-semibold text-neutral-900 dark:text-neutral-100">Materials</h2>
    Commonly made from steel and stainless steel, and can also be produced in other materials:
    </br></br>
    <ul class="list-disc pl-6 space-y-2">
      <li><strong>Carbon steel:</strong> high hardness and strength; zinc/nickel plating is often used to improve appearance and corrosion resistance.</li>
      <li><strong>Stainless steel (SUS304, 316):</strong> excellent corrosion resistance, suitable for humid/outdoor environments and industries like food, chemical, medical, and marine.</li>
      <li><strong>Aluminum alloy:</strong> lightweight with mild corrosion resistance; useful when weight reduction matters (aviation, automotive, etc.).</li>
      <li><strong>Copper:</strong> sometimes required for special applications (electronics or anti-static requirements) due to good ductility and machinability.</li>
    </ul>
